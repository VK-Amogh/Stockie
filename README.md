
# Stockei

Stockei is a fast, AI-assisted inventory and sales management app built for
small shopkeepers. It enables quick stock entry, live inventory tracking,
profit calculation, and AI-driven insights such as low-stock alerts, demand
prediction, expiry reminders, and daily summaries â€” all through a clean,
mobile-first interface designed for low digital literacy users.

 **Note:** This repository is *source-available only*. It is NOT open-source.
You may view the code, but you may not copy, modify, reuse, or redistribute it.

---

## Features -

- Fast stock entry with voice, barcode scan, and predictive autofill  
- Automatic profit and margin calculations  
- Live inventory sync and adjustments  
- AI insights:  
  - Low-stock detection  
  - Fast/slow-moving items  
  - Demand prediction  
  - Expiry reminders  
- Intuitive, minimal UI  
- Designed specifically for general stores, small shops, and retail owners
